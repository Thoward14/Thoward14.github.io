Camping in the Mountains


Back to school time was upon us and as an end to summer drew near we headed for the mountains to get away from it all for a few days and summers last break. Searching on the internet we found the Old Fo~ Camp Ground and after more investigation into the area we booked a cottage for three nights. We arrived Tuesday afternoon after a four hour drive unpacked the car and settled in. The cottage was small but we all fit, I think the most fun was had with the fire in the evening. The lake was stocked with bass and catfish although everybody caught one, none were over 4 inches in length. A lot of fun was had fishing, Mom took them out in a paddle boat and explored the lake (had to make two trips to accomodate them all), Alex was always looking for turtles! Dad, Paul, Patrick and Ian hiked all the way around the lake (we guess about 3 miles).

Some new friends were made and everyone enjoyed the Arcade and the playground. On the last night Patrick's asthma started to act up so we packed up and left at 11:00pm. We were home by 4:00am and everyone settled in for some sleep. Patrick is okay, but Mom and I both agree that the middle of the night was the way to go to avoid traffic and "Are we there yet?", although an afternoon nap would have been nice.
